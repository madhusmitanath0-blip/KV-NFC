How to Apply

Follow these clear steps to secure a successful registration for your child at Apex Academy.

Step 01

Application Inquiry

Submit your details via our online inquiry form below or pick up a physical application packet from the admin office.

Step 02

Review & Evaluation

Our admissions board reviews academic transcripts and sets a date for an interactive student evaluation exam.

Step 03

Personal Interview

Both parents and the applicant meet with the principal to align on expectation benchmarks and academic aims.

Step 04

Offer & Enrollment

Successful applicants receive an official admission offer to settle tuition dues and complete administrative registration.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are answers to the questions most commonly asked by prospective parents and students.

What are the school hours?
Primary tier classes run from 8:00 AM to 2:30 PM. Middle and High school levels operate from 8:00 AM to 3:30 PM. Extracurricular sports and club practices run from 3:45 PM to 5:00 PM on designated weeknights.
Is school bus transportation available?
Yes, we maintain a comprehensive network of air-conditioned school buses that pick up and drop off students across major metropolitan routes. Every route is equipped with GPS tracking and an on-board safety coordinator.
Are there scholarship options for outstanding students?
Apex Academy offers merit-based and sports-based scholarship discounts ranging from 10% to 50% on tuition fees for students entering Grade 9 through 12, subject to annual assessment scores and interview reviews.
What is the standard student-to-teacher ratio?
To ensure high-quality interactive learning and personal guidance, our average class size is capped at 24 students. The student-to-educator ratio is maintained at 12:1.
